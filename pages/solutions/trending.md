If you go on [Google Trends](https://trends.google.com/trends/?geo=US){:target="_blank"} and navigate in the right panel to *Year in Search*. If you look at entries from 2015 (top right corner) and select *Ghana* near the bottom of the main screen, then in the *How to* section, the number one search was **how to kiss**!

Google Trends is a website by Google that analyzes the popularity of top search queries in Google Search across various regions and languages. The website uses graphs to compare the search volume of different queries over time.

Therefore the answer is **HOW_TO_KISS**.
